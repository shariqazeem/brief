# Brief — Demo Script (3 min · evergreen)

*The agent is LIVE — its numbers and wording change every cycle. So this script **never quotes a value.** You point at the thing on screen; the words say what it *is*. Read the exact same lines on every take, whatever the agent is doing. (Only the Proof transactions are fixed — they don't change.)*

**Say "AI agent."** Slow down on three beats: *"never withdraw it" · "the chain rejected it" · "verify it yourself."* Point first, then talk — don't read the screen.

---

### 1 · COLD OPEN — face cam
> "Everyone's giving AI agents real money. Nobody asks what stops them from losing it — or running off with it.
> This is **Brief.** The AI decides what to do — the blockchain decides what it's *allowed* to do."

### 2 · ADOPT — landing → Adopt → /workforce → Adopt → sign
> "I hire one live — real USDC, one signature, Protect mode."
*(sign)*
> "Done. My money stays in my own account. It can trade it — but it can **never withdraw it.** That's a Move contract, not a promise."

### 3 · DASHBOARD — *point as you go*
> "Here's my agent, live. Up top is its thinking right now, in plain English — it updates every few seconds.
> Three things run it. The **Trader** — its read on the market, and how convinced it is. The **Risk Guardian** — a second agent watching risk every cycle. And the **chain** — zero violations, the budget enforced by Move; it can never overspend.
> And this is the custody chain: it can *trade* my money, but *never* withdraw it."

### 4 · BRAIN — *click BRAIN*
> "This is its mind — every decision, exactly how it reasoned. What it **saw.** What it **remembered.** What it **feared.** What it **decided** — and how it turned out.
> It's different every cycle. And it'll narrate any decision in plain English."

### 5 · EVOLUTION — *click EVOLUTION*
> "It learns. These are lessons it built from its own record — and they're anchored on **Walrus.** If our server vanished, a new agent reads this memory back and keeps going."

### 6 · RESULTS — *click RESULTS*
> "The scorecard — the agent versus just holding, versus sitting in cash. Every number from the real on-chain record. **Zero policy violations.**"

### 7 · PROOF — *click PROOF → click the abort tx to Suiscan*  *(the moment — fixed, won't change)*
> "And don't trust me — verify it. The kill switch: the owner revokes the agent, and the next time it tries to trade…"
*(click → Suiscan)*
> "…the chain **rejected it.** `EPolicyRevoked`. Funds untouched.
> Protocol enforcement — not a backend promise. Every claim here is a link you can check yourself."

### 8 · CLOSE — face cam
> "The AI proposes. The chain enforces.
> **Brief — the first AI agent governed by on-chain law.** Built on Sui."
